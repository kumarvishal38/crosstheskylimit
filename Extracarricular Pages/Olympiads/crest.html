What is CREST Olympiads

CREST Olympiads, which are a series of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) Olympiads for school students. The CREST Olympiads are organized by the Center for Research in Science and Technology (CREST), which is a non-profit organization that aims to promote STEM education and research among school students.
The Center for Research in Science and Technology (CREST) runs a number of competitions for school students in science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM). Some of the Olympiads conducted by CREST are:-


1. Crest Mathematics Olympiads (CMO) : The CREST Mathematics Olympiad (CMO) is an international competitive exam that tests students' knowledge and skills in mathematics. It is held online for students in grades KG-10, with one level for students in grades KG-2 and two levels for students in grades 3-10. The exam is an objective-type test that students need to complete in 60 minutes (45 minutes for KG students). It has two parts: Practical Mathematics and Achievers, and each grade has its own set of questions. The exam is conducted in English and follows the syllabus of various international boards. It is held on web-enabled devices such as laptops, tablets, or desktop computers with webcams, but mobile devices are not allowed. For students in grades KG and 1, the exam can be assisted by a parent or teacher. Students can register for the exam either individually or through their schools. The CREST Mathematics Olympiad is held annually and is open to students from any country. To be eligible for the Level 1 exam, students must be in grades KG-10, and their schools must be affiliated with a recognized education board. To be eligible for the Level 2 exam, students must have met the minimum marking criteria for Level 1, which varies each year for each grade. The exam fee for the CREST Mathematics Olympiad varies depending on the level and the mode of registration. The exam dates for the academic year 2022-23 are available on the CREST Official website.


2.Crest Science Olympiad (CSO) : The CREST Science Olympiad (CSO) is an international competitive exam that is held online for students in grades KG-10. It consists of two sections: Practical Science and Achievers Section. The exam is an objective-type test that students need to complete in 60 minutes (45 minutes for KG students). The exam is conducted in English and follows the syllabus of various international boards. It is held on web-enabled devices such as laptops, tablets, or desktop computers with webcams, but mobile devices are not allowed. For students in grades KG and 1, the exam can be assisted by a parent or teacher. Students can register for the exam either individually or through their schools. The CREST Science Olympiad is held annually and is known for its high-quality questions. To be eligible for the Level 1 exam, students must be in grades KG-10 and can be from any country. To be eligible for the Level 2 exam, students must have met the minimum marking criteria for Level 1, which varies each year for each grade. Exam Fee - INR 225


3. Crest English Olympiad (CEO) : The CREST English Olympiad (CEO) is an international exam that evaluates students' knowledge and skills in the English language. It is held online for students in grades KG-10, with one level for KG-2 and two levels for grades 3-10. The exam is an objective-type test with a specified time limit, shorter for KG students. It consists of two sections: English Language and Achievers Section. The exam is conducted in English and follows the syllabus of various international boards. Students can register individually or through their schools. To be eligible for Level 1, students must be in grades KG-10KG-10, and their schools must be affiliated with a recognized education board. To be eligible for Level 2, students must have qualified for the minimum marking criteria for Level 1. The exam fee varies based on the level and mode of registration. The exam dates for the academic year 2022-2023 can be found on the CREST website.


4.Crest Reasoning Olympiad (CRO): The CREST Reasoning Olympiad (CRO) is an international exam held online for students in grades 1-10. It is an objective-type test that students need to complete in 60 minutes. The test is the same for all grades and has only one level. It can be taken online or on paper. Students can register for the exam either individually or through their schools. The CRO is designed to test students' logical and reasoning skills. To be eligible for the exam, students must be in grades 1-10, and their schools must be affiliated with a recognized education board. The exam is open to students from any country.


5. Crest Cyber Olympiad (CCO) : The CREST Cyber Olympiad (CCO) is an international exam held online for students in grades 1-10. It is a one-level exam consisting of objective-type questions that students have 60 minutes to complete. The exam is conducted in English and follows the syllabus of various international boards, including CBSE, ICSE/ISC, and state boards. It is held on web-enabled devices such as laptops, tablets, or desktop computers with webcams, but mobile devices are not allowed. For students in the first grade, a parent or teacher can help them take the test. Students can register for the exam either individually or through their schools. The CREST Cyber Olympiad is open to students from any country. To be eligible for the exam, students must be in grades 1-10, and their schools must be affiliated with a recognized education board.


6. Crest International Spell Bee (CSB) : The CREST International Spell Bee is an online English language assessment for students in grades 1-8. It tests how well the students know the meanings, spellings, and pronunciations of English words. The exam consists of two sections: Spell Bee and Rapid Fire. It is held online, and students can register individually or through their schools. Students from any country who are enrolled in a recognized educational board, such as CBSE, ICSE, or a state board, are eligible to take the exam. To be eligible for the Level 1 exam, students must be in grades 1-8. To be eligible for the Level 2 exam, students must have met the minimum marking criteria for Level 1, which varies each year for each grade. The exam fee for the CREST International Spell Bee varies depending on the level and the mode of registration. The exam dates for the academic year 2022-23 are available on the CREST website. The syllabus for the CREST International Spell Bee includes topics such as spelling and pronunciation.
