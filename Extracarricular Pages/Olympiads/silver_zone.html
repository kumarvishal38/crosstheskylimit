What is Silver Zone Foundation Olympiads

The SilverZone Foundation conducts several Olympiads for students in different age groups and subjects. Here is a list of Olympiads organized by the SilverZone Foundation:


1.The SilverZone STEM Innovation Olympiad aims to prepare students for careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) by teaching them skills such as digital literacy, coding, computational thinking, and artificial intelligence. These skills are important for success in a rapidly changing world that relies on technology. The Olympiad is aligned with the national education policy, which emphasizes the importance of these skills for students. Eligibility - Class 3rd to class 10th More Details - https://www.silverzone.org/stem/about


2.The International Informatics Olympiad (iiO) is an exam that tests students' knowledge and skills in computer science. It is open to students from different national and international schools and is based on the curriculum of the CBSE/ICSE and various state boards. The iiO exam provides students with an opportunity to improve their computer science skills and learn about international standards in the field. Eligibility - class 1st to 12th More details - https://www.silverzone.org/iio/about


3. The International Olympiad of Mathematics (IOM) is an annual exam that tests students' knowledge and skills in mathematics. It is held at the national and international levels and is based on the curriculum of the CBSE/ICSE/ICBSE and various state boards. The IOM exam includes both standard mathematical concepts and applied mathematics and provides students with an opportunity to identify their strengths and weaknesses and strive for improvement. Eligibility - class 1st to 12th More Details - https://www.silverzone.org/iom/about


4.The International Olympiad of Science (iOS) is an annual exam that tests students' knowledge and skills in science. It is based on the curriculum of the CBSE/ICSE and various state boards and includes both standard scientific concepts and applied science. Students can find out what they are good at and what they need to work on in science by taking the iOS exam. This helps them build a strong foundation in the subject. Eligibility - Class 1st to 12th More Details - https://www.silverzone.org/ios/about


5. The International Olympiad of English Language (iOEL) is an annual exam that tests students' knowledge and skills in the English language. It is based on the curriculum of the CBSE/ICSE and various state boards, and is open to students from around the world. The iOEL provides students with an opportunity to demonstrate their proficiency in English and participate in a global competition. Eligibility - Class 1st to 12th More Details - https://www.silverzone.org/ioel/about


6.Akhil Bhartiya Hindi olympiad: The All India Hindi Olympiad is an annual exam organized by the Silverzone Foundation, a non-profit educational organization in India. It is open to students in classes 1 to 10 and aims to evaluate students' interest in and knowledge of the Hindi language. The Olympiad helps students develop skills in Hindi and prepares them for future competitions and challenges. Participating in the All India Hindi Olympiad can help students build a strong foundation in Hindi, improve their performance in exams such as the ICSE and CBSE, and prepare for state board syllabuses. It can also help students become more competent and capable of succeeding in future competitions. Eligibility - class 1st to 10th More Details - https://www.silverzone.org/abho/about


7.The exam aims to improve students' understanding of current and past events and enhance their problem-solving skills.The test is meant to help students learn more about current and past events and improve their ability to solve problems. Eligibility - Class 1st to 10th More Details - https://www.silverzone.org/skgko/about


8.The International Social Studies Olympiad (iSSO) is an annual exam that tests students' knowledge and skills in history, geography, and civics. It is held at the national and international levels and is based on the curriculum of the CBSE/ICSE and various state boards. The iSSO is meant to test how well students understand these subjects and how well they can use what they know. Eligibility - Class 1st to 10th More Details - https://www.silverzone.org/isso/about


9 . International Reasoning & Aptitude Olympiad (iRAO) is an exam that assesses students' reasoning and aptitude skills. It is designed to help students identify their strengths and weaknesses and choose the best career path for them. The Olympiad is open to students of all grades and is intended to be a comprehensive evaluation of their potential. Eligibility - Class 3rd to 12th
