List of Best International Summer Programs

1. Telluride Association programs
Eligibility - 10th and 11th class students
Cost-free, they cover all expenses
Link:- https://www.tellurideassociation.org/


2. YYGS
Eligibility - Sophomores and Juniors (grades 10 and 11), age at least 16
Cost-Approximately $6,000, which includes accommodation, meals, and program activities. This does not include travel expenses, which students will need to cover separately.
Link:- https://apply.globalscholars.yale.edu/apply/


3. RSI(MIT)
Eligibility - high school juniors, and seniors are not eligible
Cost-free
Link:- https://www.cee.org/programs/apply-rsi


4. Anson L. Clark Scholars Program
Eligibility - must be at least 17 years of age by the program start date
Cost-Free and also receive $750 stipend(they don't cover travel expenses)
Link:- https://www.depts.ttu.edu/honors/academicsandenrichment/affiliatedandhighschool/clarks/


5. Young Scholars Programme - Ashoka University
Eligibility - 9,10,11,12th Grade
Cost-INR 25,000/- (inclusive of taxes)
Link:- https://www.ashoka.edu.in/academic-programme/young-scholar-programme/


5. Young Scholars Programme - Ashoka University
Eligibility - 9,10,11,12th Grade
Cost-INR 25,000/- (inclusive of taxes)
Link:- https://www.ashoka.edu.in/academic-programme/young-scholar-programme/


6.SSP (Summer Science Program)
Eligibility - 10th and 11th-grade students (aged 15-18)
Cost- 7950 US dollars (covers room and board, tuition, local transportation, other supplies) Financial Aid available
Link:- https://summerscience.org/admissions/fee-and-financial-aid/


7.Ross Mathematics Program
Eligibility - Age- 15 - 18, high school students
Cost- 1500 US dollars(Online)
Link:- https://rossprogram.org/


8.SUMMER@INSEAD
Eligibility - 15 - 18-year-old high school students
Cost- 6825 US dolloas(Online)
Link:- https://www.insead.edu/summer-at-insead


9.INSEAD STARTUP PROGRAM
Eligibility - 15 - 18-year-old high school students
Cost- 1228 US dollars(Online)
Link:- https://www.insead.edu/summer-at-insead/startup


10.SUMAC ( Stanford university mathematics camp ) online program
Eligibility - Juniors and seniors from around the world for intensive study in advanced mathematics.
Cost- 3250 US dollars(Online)
Link:- https://sumac.spcs.stanford.edu/sumac-about


11. ISSYP (Perimeter Institute)
Eligibility - Age - 16-18, class - 10th, 11th, 12th, Dropper
Cost- 325 CAD
Link:- https://perimeterinstitute.ca/issyp


12. Harvard Summer School( Pre-college program)
Eligibility - Age 16-18
Cost- 4950 US Dollar
