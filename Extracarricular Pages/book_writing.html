How to write a book:-

Step 1:-Choose your topic(Choose a topic that matches your audience needs).

Step 2:- Plan your structure with solution in your mind.

Step 3:- Choose Your writing platform.

Step 4;- Break down each chapter as you write.

Trick & Tips to write a successful book:-

  1. Use right colour
  2. Outline each chapter of your book
  3. Develop a landing page
  4. Design the book template & cover
  5. Edit to a professional level.
  6. Do your research as you can.
  7. Choose your deadline & writing goal
  8. Prepare a draft before writing

How to design a book cover

  1. Have a look at the covers of best selling books to see what makes the good one work,it's colour,image,font
  2. Make your title big, bold & easy to read
  3. Use unsplash website to get top high quality free image
  4. Share your book design with your friend ,family & again true suggestion.
  5. Congrats! You're finally ready to publish your book
  6. Enter your title,book description & keywords
  7. Upload your manuscript as on EPUB file
  8. Add your book cover[JPG,PNG or PDF]
  9. Price your book & hit “Publish”

Note:- Many websites allow you to publish for free like Amazon Kindle

Fastest way to write book:-

  1. Write your first draft: This can take a month to on year,depending upon your book,& life comitment
  2. Get Feedback: Share up your book with family members & friend to read it
  3. Editing(at least two weeks) : Use your family & friend feedback and edit once more
  4. Add your book detail

Thing you need to publish on e-book-

  • Book title:- Your book title should make your content with awesome title & book cover.
  • Author name :- Your name
  • Description:- This is the bit of blurb that readers see when they browse your book(and consider buying it).Make it really good.
  • Rights :- You own all rights & choose to sell your book anywhere in the world.
