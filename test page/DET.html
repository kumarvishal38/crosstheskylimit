SOME OF THE QUESTIONS OFTEN ASKED—


Ques. Is Duolingo accepted for USA student visa?

Ans. - Yes, Duolingo English tests are accepted for USA student visa applications. There are over 100 universities in USA that accept DET. Even after the pandemic, the universities are still accepting Duolingo as proof of proficiency.


Ques. Is Duolingo easier than IELTS?

Ans. The difficulty level of the tests is based on the knowledge of the students about the English language. Duolingo tests are shorter in duration than IELTS.


Ques. Is 100 Duolingo score accepted in USA?

Ans. The minimum score for Duolingo tests varies by university. The average DET score requirement for USA universities lies between 100-150. However, the top universities generally accept DET scores above 120.


Ques. How long is Duolingo test valid?

Ans. Duolingo tests are valid for 2 years.


Ques. Does Harvard accept Duolingo?

Ans. Duolingo is accepted in Harvard Extension School with a minimum requirement of 125. Harvard University does not accept Duolingo for its mainstream courses.


Ques. How many times can I retake Duolingo test?

Ans. Students can attempt the Duolingo test twice a month and not more than that. However, they can also try again in the next month.


Ques. Can I convert Duolingo to IELTS?

es. Duolingo scores can be easily converted to IELTS. A DET score of 85-90 is equivalent to an IELTS score of 5.5. On the other hand, a DET score of 115-120 is equivalent to an IELTS score of 7.0


Take free practice exams available


After you sign up for the test, you will have the access to many practice exams and you can take the test as many times as you want to take it. Practising from the practice test is highly recommended as it helps you to understand the structure of the exam, which includes the nature of question types and the time limit given. As each test gives you a different set of questions through which you will be able to understand the type of questions and will feel comfortable while giving the final test.


Improve your accent and pronunciation


To work on your speaking skills, you must focus on speaking clearly and understanding the pronunciation, intonation and stress of syllables. Improve your pronunciation by practicing longer sentences and ensuring that you don’t break the flow while speaking with a clear focus on every syllable or stress in different words.


Sharpen your vocabulary and grammar


Grammar and vocabulary are the constitutions of every language. The key to sharpening your grammar is to practice them through different types of questions and writing. You must also read as much as you can to better your vocabulary and create flashcards to learn new words every day.


Stay open to feedback


Once you have started practicing your speaking, writing, reading and listening in English, you must always stay open to feedback. Have conversations with those who have a good grasp of English and ask them to give you notes on how you can be better. For speaking skills, you can record yourself and get feedback from others and for writing skills, try writing articles, short passage, entries

ON DAY OF EXAM YOU NEED TO CARE ABOUT :-


• A photo ID issued by the government (passport, driver’s license or government ID)

• A quiet area with good lighting.

• An uninterrupted period of more than one hour.

• A computer with a built-in front-facing webcam, speakers, and microphone (no earphones are allowed to prevent cheating).

• A strong internet connection and a browser that works with the test.


Duolingo English Test rules:


• You must be alone in the room (which must be well lit).

• Your ears must be visible and uncovered.

• Your face must be visible and unobscured.

• No headphones or earphones.

• No outside materials, including mobile devices, notes, or textbooks.

• No communication with anyone else is permitted.

• No writing utensils or paper are permitted.

• You must not look away from the screen for any significant period.

• You aren’t permitted to leave the test window on your web browser for any reason.
